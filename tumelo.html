Tumelo

Born in Bethlehem and raised in the Vaal, Tumelo Ntja is a multidisciplinary visual artist and creative artist whose career is rooted in a lifelong fascination with the arts. Originally a self-taught illustrator and painter, Tumelo's early ambition to reach the 'top arena' of the art world pushed him to realise his full potential.

While his early work focused exclusively on traditional mediums, his university years marked a significant turning point where he transitioned into a professional, selling custom student portraits on campus. Today, he has expanded his creative repertoire to include photography, cinematography, and street wear design, blending a background in Marketing Management with a deep passion for visual storytelling.

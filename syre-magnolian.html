Syre Magnolian

Bongani Lebohang Koena Professionally Know as Syre Magnolian is a Versatile artist known for his unique sound and captivating Performances. Born and raised in Vaal, Sebokeng , Syre Magnolian began his musical journey at a young age,drawing inspiration from Micheal Jackson, Whitney Houston, Drake, Chris Brown, Travis Scott, Xxxtentacion and many more.

Music Style
Syre Magnolian's music blends Vaal Italian Raps with His Unique Melodic flows that creates a signature sound that resonates with The Magnolians(his fans).

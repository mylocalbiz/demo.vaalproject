About

The Project

This interactive documentary is structured around four key themes drawn from the capability approach, which guide both the collection and interpretation of data. These themes—environmental, social, psychological, and physical capability—provide a framework for understanding the different factors that shape an individual's potential to succeed as a social media influencer. Throughout the documentary, each theme is explored through participant interviews, offering insight into how these capabilities are experienced and developed in real contexts. By engaging with these perspectives, the documentary seeks to highlight which capabilities and practices are most effective in supporting the growth and success of emerging influencers.

Meet the Creator

Thabo Makhoana

Born and raised in Sebokeng Zone 7, Vaal Triangle.

I am a photographer, cinematographer, and music composer, working across visual and sound-based storytelling practices. My work is rooted in a deep love for visual arts and collaborative creation.

I enjoy working closely with artists, using image, motion, and sound to tell stories that reflect lived experiences, identity, and place. Alongside my creative practice, I also work as an artist manager, supporting emerging creatives in shaping their careers and navigating the creative industry.

Through my work, I am committed to creating meaningful narratives that highlight local talent, amplify creative voices, and contribute to the evolving cultural landscape of the Vaal.

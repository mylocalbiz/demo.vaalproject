Paul

Goitsemodimo Melato, known professionally as Paul is a Problem, is a Sebokeng-based multidisciplinary skateboarder, coach, and community practitioner. He has earned widespread recognition for a dynamic style that fuses technical precision with creative flair, underpinned by a deep-rooted commitment to youth empowerment.

Moving beyond his personal achievements on the board, Paul serves as a vital mentor within his community. He leverages skateboarding as a transformative tool to instill discipline, foster self-confidence, and promote social cohesion among the next generation.
